Biography of Gandhi Ji :-

He is known as Father of nation. His full name is Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi. His parents were Karamchand Gandhi and Putlibai. We already know that he was born in porbandar at Gujarat. He was so simple and modest since childhood. He married at the age of 13 with Kasturba Gandhi. After that he went to South Africa to get the education of law. But he saw that there was totally the misbehaves with blacks and have a feel of proud of being white africans. This was an great incident with him in South Africa where he was thrown off from the train while sitting in a third class of whites. Then he decided to fight against this untouchability with two other colours in a same country. Gandhiji started a movement after returning from south africa named Savvinay Avagya movement. In this movement, he went with his disciples and revolutionaries to fight against whites and blacks and leave all orders of Britishers. He also held some other movements like Non-Cooperation movement ,Leave British India movement, Break salt law, and khilafat movement. He believed in truth and non-violence. So his weapons were Truth and Non-Violence. But in Non-cooperation movement , is disciples were violented and brought fire to prison of a place named Chaura- Chauri in Uttar pradesh and there were 22 policemen died. This incident hurted him a lot, and force him to go back with it. Then he started living in small huts of lower caste people and serving him for sometime. At last, he was shooted with the gun of a hinduism hating guy named Nathuram Godse and died in few minutes on 30 january, 1948. He made our country free from British Rule forever.
